## Definition

The **cognitive lightcone** is not the distance a system can sense or reach with its actuators. Rather, it is the size of the largest goal a system can pursue in some problem space.

As systems become more complex and integrated (from single cells → multicellular tissues → organisms → collectives), their cognitive lightcone enlarges. They gain the capacity to pursue larger, more complex, longer-horizon goals.

## Why This Matters

This reframes how we think about scaling intelligence and agency:

- **Not about connectivity:** Intelligence doesn't scale simply by adding more neurons or more cells
- **About goal complexity:** Intelligence scales when a system can hold and pursue increasingly large/complex goals
- **Unifies biology and AI:** Whether a system is biological or artificial, what matters is the size of goals it can coherently pursue

The cognitive lightcone explains why a single cell has some minimal form of intelligence (pursuing homeostatic goals) while a human can pursue abstract, long-horizon, counterfactual goals (saving money for retirement, building a career, understanding the universe).

## The Scaling Mechanism: "Cognitive Glue"

Levin uses the term "cognitive glue" to describe mechanisms that allow cells to integrate across larger regions of space and time, thereby enlarging the collective's cognitive lightcone:

### Gap Junctions and Electrical Coupling

When cells connect via **gap junctions** (proteinaceous pores allowing small molecules and ions to flow between cells), something remarkable happens:

- **Chemical signals** between unconnected cells are clearly "from outside" — easy to ignore or reinterpret
- **Electrical signals** through gap junctions enter the internal milieu — the receiving cell **cannot tell** if an event was from itself or another cell sharing the same electrical state

This creates a **"mind meld"** effect:
- Memory anonymization — shared memories are indistinguishable from individual memories
- The collective can now integrate information over spatial and temporal scales set by the propagation speed of electrical signals
- This expansion of shared information space = expansion of possible goal size

### Stress Sharing

Another form of cognitive glue mentioned: physical stress propagates through tissues, creating collective awareness of stress patterns. This allows the tissue collective to sense and respond to events at scales larger than any individual cell.

## Implications

1. **Medicine:** To regenerate limbs or repair birth defects, we need to expand the cognitive lightcone of cell collectives — get them to pursue "whole body" goals rather than local defaults
2. **AI:** This suggests neural networks might not be the only path to scaling intelligence; any system that can integrate goals across scales might achieve agency
3. **Philosophy:** There is no sharp boundary between "minded" and "non-minded" systems — it's a continuum determined by the size of goals pursued

## Open Questions

- What is the relationship between cognitive lightcone size and consciousness/qualia?
- Can we measure cognitive lightcone empirically?
- What hard limits exist on how large a cognitive lightcone can become?
- Is there a cognitive lightcone for humanity as a whole? For the internet as an emergent system?

## Cross-Domain Connections

**Neuroscience:** The concept mirrors research on neural integration and global workspace theory — the idea that consciousness arises from large-scale information integration across brain regions.

**Information Theory:** Cognitive lightcone is a description of coherent information integration across a system. Related to Integrated Information Theory (IIT) and measures of system-wide coherence.

**Complex Systems:** The scaling of goals mirrors phase transitions in complex systems. At certain thresholds of integration, qualitatively new behaviors and capabilities emerge.

**AI Safety:** If AI systems have expanding cognitive lightcones, then as they become more capable, their goals become harder to predict or control — a key safety concern.
